Nestled within a dense, unyielding northern forest, **Iceham** is a fortified military village rather than a bustling city. The settlement is tightly packed, with its wooden and stone-roofed longhouses clustered together for warmth and defense against the harsh winter climate. Surrounded entirely by a thick canopy of frost-resistant trees, the village feels isolated from the rest of the world, serving as a strategic outpost and a penal settlement on the edge of the frontier. 
### General Overview
* **Population:** Around 250–300 residents. The community is small and insular, composed mostly of garrisoned soldiers, prison wardens, and local woodcutters who clear the surrounding wilderness.
* **Wealth:** Low. Because it is a remote village, resources are strictly rationed. The crown funds the military presence and the high-security prison, but the wealth is kept tightly within the garrison, leaving the common folk to live a subsistence lifestyle.
* **Primary Industry:** Penal Guarding, Woodcutting, and Border Patrol.
### Geography and Layout
* **The Village Cluster:** There is nothing glamorous or extraordinary about Iceham. It is a plain, rustic settlement where residential quarters are heavily concentrated in a central clearing. Narrow, winding dirt paths snake between closely built homes, creating a claustrophobic and easily monitored environment.
* **The Outskirts and Fields:** To the northwest of the village lies a small cleared clearing featuring a tiny body of water and cleared plots of land utilized for basic, cold-weather farming and livestock gathering. 
* **The Surrounding Wilds:** The village is utterly engulfed by a massive, deep forest that acts as a natural wall, making escape from the settlement or its prison near impossible without being tracked. It provides endless timber for the local woodcutters who make up the majority of the civilian populace.
### Government and Key Figures

#### Mayor Thaddeus Vance
In a small village like Iceham, **Mayor Thaddeus Vance** acts more like a weary village elder or magistrate. He handles the daily complaints of the few local families and woodcutters, manages food stores, and tracks civilian trade. Lacking any real physical power, he is entirely subservient to the military presence and lives in constant anxiety of angering the garrison.

#### City Commander (The Guard Captain)
The true ruler of Iceham is the **City Commander**, the brutal captain who oversees both the village garrison and the high-security prison cells (hidden within or just beneath the settlement). He rules this small community with absolute martial law. In a village of this size, his oppressive presence is felt by every single inhabitant on a daily basis.
