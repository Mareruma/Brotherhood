## Backstory
**Fin** is a noble youth born into the prestigious Brightwater family, one of the realm's wealthiest dynasties whose vast fortune was built upon a sprawling monopoly over agriculture and food production. Despite the family's immense riches, Fin's upbringing was defined by isolation and cruelty; because his mother was a common prostitute, making his noble blood inherit only from his father, he was treated as a shameful secret and forced to live in the damp confines of the estate's cellar. Nevertheless, his lineage afforded him a high-quality education, sharpening his mind even as his family broke his spirit. Driven by a righteous desire to rise above the filth of his upbringing and the malice of his kin, Fin swore sacred oaths to become a holy paladin, using his privilege and newfound martial devotion to champion justice and shield others from the kind of cruelty he endured in the dark.
