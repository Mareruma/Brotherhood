## Backstory
**Dain** is a rugged Hill Dwarf whose existence is tied to a secluded settlement where he was raised alongside his father and his eighteen brothers. This massive brotherhood represents the sole inhabitants of their entire village, living in complete isolation from the outside world. The household was founded upon a profound family tragedy, as their mother did not survive the grueling toll of bringing her nineteen sons into the world. Deep within their vacant enclave, Dain and his kin serve as the solitary guardians of a sacred tree, a mystical monument central to their family's purpose. Growing up amidst the unique camaraderie of his identical brothers, Dain developed a fierce sense of loyalty, enduring patience, and the unbreakable grit of his heritage, forever bound to the protection of his home and the holy timber within it.
