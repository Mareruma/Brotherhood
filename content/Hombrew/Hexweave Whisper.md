*Weapon (firearm), legendary (Requires proficiency with firearms)*

This sleek, matte-black anti-material rifle bridges the gap between modern military precision and dark arcane magic. Crafted from light-absorbing shadowsteel, its surface remains cold to the touch and never reflects light. A massive suppressor wrapped in glowing silence runes ensures that its lethal strikes are never heard, only felt.

You have a **+3 bonus** to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. It possesses the following magical properties:

***Arcane Scope.*** While looking through the rifle's quad-lens holographic scope, you gain **Truesight** out to a range of 120 feet and can cast *Detect Magic* at will, without expending a spell slot or components. 

***Ghost Ammunition.*** The rifle does not require physical ammo. Pulling the bolt back automatically materializes a bolt of pure shadow energy. Once per turn, you can choose to make a shot phase through solid matter. This attack ignores half cover and three-quarter cover, and can pass through up to 5 feet of solid stone, wood, or metal to hit a target behind it.

***Mark of the Hunter.*** As a bonus action, you can lock onto a creature you can see through the scope. For the next 24 hours, you know the creature's exact location as long as it is on the same plane of existence, up to a maximum range of 1 mile.

***Dead or Alive.*** When you hit a creature with an attack from this weapon, you can choose to activate one of the following infusions:
* **Dead (Necrotic):** The shot detonates with a sickly green flame upon impact. The target takes an additional **3d10 necrotic damage**.
* **Alive (Stasis):** The shot deals no damage, but instead dissolves into binding threads of arcane shadow. The target must succeed on a **DC 17 Wisdom saving throw** or be paralyzed for 1 minute. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.

***Interrogate the Fallen.*** If a creature is slain by this weapon, its soul is trapped within the scope's crystal lens for 24 hours. During this time, you can use an action to ask the soul up to three questions, as per the *Speak with Dead* spell. The soul cannot lie to its killer. Once used, this property cannot be used again until the next dawn, and any trapped soul is released to the afterlife.
