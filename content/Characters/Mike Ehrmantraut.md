## Backstory
**Mike** is a disciplined High Elf who bridges the lethal boundary between a rogue bounty hunter and a newly christened assassin. Gifted with an unnatural patience and an unerring eye, he mastered the art of long-range marksman under the guidance of a trusted criminal contact. However, on the very eve of completing his grueling training, Mike was betrayed and cast into the depths of a high-security military prison. For twenty agonizing years, the long-lived elf endured the darkness, his youthful ambition hardening into a cold, unbreakable hatred. His liberation finally came at a grim price: the prison’s commanding Military Captain offered him freedom in exchange for the life of the Captain's own niece. Mike accepted the contract to secure his release and reclaim his rifle, yet beneath his compliant veneer lies a silent, lethal vow. He will hunt his target, but his ultimate vengeance is reserved for the Captain who stole two decades of his life.
