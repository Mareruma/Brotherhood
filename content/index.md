title: Brotherhood of the Damned
---
**The Brotherhood of the Damned** is a dangerous and highly controversial adventuring party. They are not the heroes people welcome with flowers and applause—more often, their arrival is met with slammed shutters and anxious whispers. Society fears them and deeply distrusts their grim methods, yet no one can deny their brutal efficiency. They have survived horrors that would break lesser men, forging them into a veteran force. When a situation becomes utterly hopeless and traditional heroes turn their backs, everyone knows exactly where to look for them.
